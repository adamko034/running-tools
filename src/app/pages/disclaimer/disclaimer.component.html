Not Medical Advice

Our calculators do not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

Health Conditions & Contraindications

Do NOT use our calculators if you have:

  • Heart disease, cardiovascular conditions, or chest pain
  • High blood pressure or other circulatory problems
  • Diabetes or blood sugar disorders
  • Respiratory conditions (asthma, COPD, etc.)
  • Joint, bone, or muscle injuries or disorders
  • Eating disorders or history of disordered eating
  • Pregnancy or recent childbirth
  • Any chronic medical condition
  • Take medications that affect heart rate or metabolism

When to Seek Medical Attention

Stop exercising immediately and seek medical help if you experience:

  • Chest pain, pressure, or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Dizziness, lightheadedness, or fainting
  • Irregular heartbeat or palpitations
  • Nausea or vomiting during exercise
  • Severe joint or muscle pain
  • Any unusual or concerning symptoms
