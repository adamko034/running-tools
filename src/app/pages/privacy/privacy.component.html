Introduction

Welcome to Runner Toolkit. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our running calculators and tools.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection and use of your information and tells you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our tools and user experience. The analytics data we collect includes:

  • Device and browser information (type, version, screen resolution)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, click patterns)
  • Performance metrics (page load times, errors)
  • General location data (country/city level, not precise location)

Local Storage Data

Our website stores some information locally on your device to enhance your experience. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Your unit preferences (metric vs imperial)
  • Recent calculation inputs for convenience
  • Language and display settings

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. These ads help us keep our running tools free for everyone to use.

How AdSense Uses Cookies

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. These cookies enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Personalized Advertising

Google may use your data to personalize ads shown to you. You can control personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings page.

open_in_new Manage Ad Preferences

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage statistics and improve our website performance. This service helps us understand how users interact with our running calculators.

Data Collected by Firebase

  • User interactions and events (button clicks, page views, calculator usage)
  • Demographics and interests (age range, gender, interests - when available)
  • User behavior patterns (session duration, bounce rate, popular tools)
  • Crash reports and performance data to improve stability

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

open_in_new Download Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our running calculator tools and website functionality
  • To understand how users interact with our tools and identify popular features
  • To serve relevant advertisements that help keep our tools free
  • To detect and resolve technical issues and improve website performance
  • To ensure website security and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity

Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, we process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate Interest: We process data to improve our website, analyze usage patterns, and provide better user experience
  • Consent: For cookies and tracking technologies, we rely on your consent through our cookie banner
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide our free running calculator services and maintain website functionality

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share limited data with trusted third-party services to operate our website:

  • Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics for website usage analysis and improvement
  • Google AdSense for serving relevant advertisements to keep our tools free
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety

International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where Google's servers are located.

Transfer Safeguards

  • EU-US Data Privacy Framework for transfers to the United States
  • Google's compliance with international data protection standards and contractual safeguards

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing of your personal data
